Phase 1 - Technical

       
ED - Engineering Design

The elevator system will cover at least three floors, with one CAN node per floor (“call-station”).

Each call-station will have up/down switches and lights

The elevator car will have door open/close switches & lights

The elevator car will floor-selection switches & lights

The elevator car will have LCD/7-segment display for displaying current location of the elevator.

Detect the location of the elevator car (with the distance sensor) and distribute this information to all call-stations for displaying on LCD/7-segment display

Detect up/down request at any floor and switch on the respective LED

Detect floor selection at any floor and switch on the respective LED

Detect door open/close request at any floor and switch on the respective LED

Include maximum hardware aids for debugging including test points, LEDs, start/stop switches, analog input knob

Provide for future reuse of software and hardware in subsequent projects of the term

New features for the elevator operation (to be considered above and beyond).

Detect door open/close request at any floor and switch on the respective LED

Tests the elevator system to ensure it covers at least three floors, with one CAN node per floor (“call-station”).

Tests the up/down switches and lights of each call station

Tests the open/close switches & lights of the elevator car

Test the floor-selection switches & lights of the elevator car

Tests the LCD/7-segment display to ensure it displays the current location of the elevator.

Tests the systems capability to detect the location of the elevator car (with the distance sensor) as well as its abaility to distribute this information to all call-stations for displaying on LCD/7-segment display

Tests to ensure the system can detect up/down request at any floor and switch on the respective LED

Tests to ensure the system can detect floor selection at any floor and switch on the respective LED

Tests to ensure the system can detect a door open/close request at any floor and switch on the respective LED
